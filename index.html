Info about me

I first got exposed to computers at an early age. I tried out many things like watching tech movies, typing practice, painting and playing games: Space Impact and Spider Solitaire were my best before I upgraded to GTA and Need for Speed!

I then got curious about how the computer itself works and began learning how to program. I also reached the extent of breaking computers apart to see where their magic comes from. This led me to diving deeper into ICT and enrolling for Software Engineering at university.

Fast forward, I have tested various capabilities of different types of computers and experimented on a number of programming languages through designing programs. The software industry evolves by the second, and I try my best to stay updated with the current trends while cementing my knowledge in the fundamentals.

My goal is to contribute to the enhancement of society through engaging in the advancement of technology that will benefit humanity. I love open source for it aligns with my passion of software for all.
